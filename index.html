Why Zook?

Your new kitchen should excite you! The process of designing your kitchen should be an enjoyable and comfortable experience.  We are a husband and wife team with a talented, professional staff, completely dedicated to you.  Our service and personalized attention allows us to achieve our goal of making your kitchen renovation a smooth and pleasurable experience. Please see client reviews of our work at Houzz.

Greg has over 30 years of experience in the kitchen industry.  In 2001, we started Zook Kitchens.  We offer full service remodeling of your kitchen.  From design work to installation, with changes to flooring, electrical and plumbing needs, our full team is available to you.

Our clients choose us because they are confident that we will manage the renovation process and use only experienced professional craftsmen on their project.  We are extremely proud to sell, exclusively, cabinetry made in the USA because it does matter.
